The **Billing** page provides a comprehensive overview of your TempusMail subscription and financial transactions.

## Current Subscription

The top section displays your active plan details:
- **Plan Type**: Basic, Professional, or Enterprise.
- **User Count**: Number of licensed seats.
- **Amount**: Recurring cost per period (Monthly/Yearly).
- **Status**: Active or Inactive.
- **Payment Dates**: Start date, End date, and Next Payment date.
- **Payment Method**: The card or service (e.g., Stripe, Zoho) used for billing.

## Managing Subscription

Click the **Manage Subscription** button to be redirected to the secure portal where you can:
- Upgrade or Downgrade your plan.
- Update payment methods.
- Download official invoices.

## Transaction History

A list of all past transactions is available, showing:
- Transaction ID
- Amount & Currency
- Status (Completed, Pending, Failed)
- Date processed

*For billing support, please contact our dedicated financial support team.*
