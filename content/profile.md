The **Profile** page allows you to view and manage your personal administrator account details.

## Personal Information
- **Avatar**: Your profile picture.
- **Name & Email**: Your display name and verified email address.
- **Custom Domain Email**: Displays if you have an email associated with a custom domain.

## Contact Details
- **Phone Number**: Contact number on file.
- **Member Since**: Account creation date.
- **Address**: Registered physical address for the account owner.

## Subscription Summary
A quick view of your subscription status is also available here, mirroring the information on the Billing page for convenience.

*To update sensitive information like passwords or email addresses, you may need to re-authenticate for security purposes.*
