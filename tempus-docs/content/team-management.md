The **Team Members** section allows you to manage administrators and other users who have access to the TempusMail control panel.

## Viewing Team Members

Navigate to **Dashboard > Team Members**.
You will see a list of cards representing each team member, displaying:
- **Name & Email**: Information about the user.
- **Status**: Enabled (Active) or Disabled.
- **Role/Permissions**: Badges indicating what actions they can perform.
- **Codes**: User code and Customer code.

## Permissions

Team members can have granular permissions assigned to them, such as:
- `manage_domains`: Ability to create/edit domains.
- `manage_billing`: Access to billing information and invoices.
- `view_reports`: Read-only access to system reports.
- `manage_users`: Ability to add or remove other team members.

## Searching

Use the search or filter functionality to find specific team members in large organizations.
